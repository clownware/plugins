# Note on `_ds_manifest.json`, `_ds_bundle.js`, `_adherence.oxlintrc.json`

These three files are generated by Claude Design on import (namespace
`PezzaDesignSystem_8fc740`) and regenerate on re-import — do not hand-edit them
expecting the edits to survive a round-trip.

During the chassis-parity enhancement they received mechanical find/replace
updates only (token renames, `hsl()` wraps, WebP asset paths) so that previews
stay consistent with the refactored tokens until the next re-import. The new
components (MetaLabel, PhotoHero) carry `@startingPoint` annotations in their
`.d.ts` files and will be picked up by the manifest on re-import; they are not
hand-registered here.
