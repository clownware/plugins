Mixing in key, explained

Harmonic mixing is just interval math dressed up for the booth. Two tracks a fifth apart share most of their scale tones, so the blend holds.

The crossfader is an argument between two truths. Win it slowly.

const next = camelot("8A").adjacent();
// ["7A", "9A", "8B"] - safe moves
