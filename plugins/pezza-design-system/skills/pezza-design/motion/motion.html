The X is a crossfader

Four primitives. Every one collapses to its final state (or a plain fade) under reduced motion. No infinite loops in product UI - the self-running playhead sweep below is marked as a demo context.

draw-on

The mark draws as one continuous stroke - 82.12 units of path, 900ms, --ease-out. Multiple paths stagger by 140ms via --draw-index.

crossfade-wipe

Panels enter behind a moving vertical edge - the crossfader move. --ease-crossfade, --dur-slow. The edge is ink; the accent never decorates transitions.

Slide-over content

Wipes in from the left edge, exits to the right.

playhead

A thin accent sweep along one edge of the now-playing element, driven by a registered @property. One per view, max. In product, drive it from real progress; the looping sweep is demo-only.
